## What is aiwave

aiwave is an AI-powered sound effect generation platform. Instead of searching through stock libraries, you describe the sound you need — like *"a cyberpunk UI chime with digital glitch texture"* or *"distant thunder during a heavy rainstorm, rain hitting a glass window"* — and the AI generates it in seconds.

Traditional sound libraries answer the question: *"does this sound exist?"*  
aiwave answers: *"can I create exactly the sound I imagine?"*
